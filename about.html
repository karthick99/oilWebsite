Rooted in Tradition

Senthoor Oils was born from a simple desire: to bring the purity of the farm directly to your table, while empowering the hands that grow it.

Our Mission

We believe that good health starts with good food. In a world full of processed and adulterated products, we stand for purity.

Our mission is twofold:

  • ✓ To provide 100% natural, chemical-free products to our customers.
  • ✓ To ensure fair trade and sustainable livelihoods for our partner farmers.

The Farmer Connection

We don't deal with middlemen. We work directly with farmers in Tamil Nadu who practice organic farming methods. This ensures that every drop of oil you consume is traceable, ethical, and pure.

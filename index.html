Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our organic oils

What makes your oils different from store-bought oils?

Our oils are 100% cold-pressed using traditional wooden chekku methods, preserving all natural nutrients and flavor. We source directly from organic farmers in Tamil Nadu, ensuring zero chemicals, no preservatives, and complete traceability from farm to bottle.

How long do the oils stay fresh?

When stored in a cool, dark place, our oils remain fresh for 6-9 months. We recommend refrigeration after opening for maximum freshness. Each bottle is marked with a production date for your reference.
